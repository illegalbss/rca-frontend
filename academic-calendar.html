Key Dates & Events

September 8, 2025

First Term Resumption

All pupils resumed for the first term of the 2025/2026 academic session.

December 12, 2025

First Term Ends

Pupils proceeded on holiday. Report cards issued to parents at end-of-term assembly.

January 12, 2026

Second Term Resumption

Pupils returned for the second term. School fees for the term were due on resumption day.

February 16 — 20, 2026

Mid-Term Test Week

Continuous assessment tests for all classes, Pre-Nursery to Basic 6.

April 3, 2026

Second Term Ends

Examinations concluded. Report cards distributed before the short holiday began.

April 27, 2026

Third Term Resumption

Pupils resumed for the final term of the 2025/2026 academic session.

July 24, 2026

Third Term Ends — Prize Giving Day

Academic session concludes with the annual Prize Giving Day ceremony and long holiday begins.
