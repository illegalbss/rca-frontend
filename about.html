Home › About Royal Crystal Academy

Our Mission

To provide quality, well-rounded primary education that develops academic excellence, strong character, and digital literacy in every pupil.

Our Vision

To be a leading primary school known for nurturing confident, curious, and responsible young learners ready for the digital future.

Our Core Values

Discipline, Excellence, Integrity, Respect, and Innovation guide everything we do — in the classroom and beyond.

Our Story

Royal Crystal Academy was established to serve families in our community with a focus on quality, affordable education from Pre-Nursery through to Basic 6.

Today we serve approximately 230 pupils, supported by a dedicated team of teachers and staff. We are proud to be one of the few primary schools in the area with a fully equipped ICT laboratory, helping our pupils build digital skills from an early age.

Our approach combines the standard national curriculum with practical, hands-on learning — including weekly computer lessons for every class and an after-school Coding Club for interested pupils.
