How to Apply

1

Fill the Form

Complete the application form below with your child's details.

2

Submission Review

Our admissions team reviews your application within 3 working days.

3

Assessment

Your child may be invited for a brief, friendly assessment visit.

4

Confirmation

You'll receive an admission letter and fee schedule to complete enrollment.

Admission Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Child's birth certificate (original + copy)
  • 2 recent passport photographs of the child
  • Previous school report (for transfer pupils)
  • Parent/Guardian valid ID
