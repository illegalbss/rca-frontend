Official fee structure for all pupils. Fees are the same across all classes. The sibling discount applies automatically when a family has 3 or more children currently enrolled.

👪 Sibling Discount Policy

When a family has 3 or more children simultaneously enrolled at Royal Crystal Academy, the school fees component is reduced from ₦30,000 to ₦15,000 per child. All other fees (Utility Bill, Lesson, and term levies) remain at full price.
